§01 · Why we built this

The open index the segment didn't have.

Cliffwater's CDLI-LMM, launched September 2024, is the institutional benchmark for the segment — borrowers averaging less than $40M EBITDA, BDC-universe, US-only, subscription-only. It's the right comparison for Studio b. LMMI to be measured against.

Studio b. LMMI takes a different access model on a partially-overlapping universe. Methodology is open and published. The headline quarterly print and weekly market commentary are free. Detailed institutional data — full constituent universe, historical CSV, DD-grade brief — will be subscription-priced for researchers, journalists, and allocators. Verified accredited LPs access the backtester through invest.b.studio.

Coverage extends beyond the BDC universe. SBA 7(a) loan data captures sub-BDC origination volume. UK Companies House workout records anchor cross-jurisdictional recovery rates. The methodology paper, the universe construction rules, and the calibration scope are all on lmmi.b.studio/methodology.
