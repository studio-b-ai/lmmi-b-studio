Why we built this

The benchmarks didn't exist.

Lower middle market direct lending is the largest under-benchmarked corner of credit. The big indices track upper-middle and broadly syndicated — Cliffwater's CDLI is filtered to the BDC universe, which is structurally upper-middle. The data sources LPs use to underwrite LMM allocators are surveys, self-reports, and selective marks. Every layer between the deal and the LP filters out the inconvenient.

So we built the index we wished existed: quarterly spread, structure, and recovery data sourced from SBA 7(a), Companies House workouts, and the LMM-eligible universe. Methodology is open. Universe is documented. Nothing is paid-promoted.

Read the data free. Subscribers get the methodology, the universe construction notes, and market-event commentary as it happens. Accredited LPs interested in the fund can request the deck.
